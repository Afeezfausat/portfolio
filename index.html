    • Get To Know Me

    I am an entry-level Data and Actuarial analyst with a background in actuarial science and practical experience in insurance and claims analytics. Proficient in Excel, SQL, Power BI, and statistics for data cleaning, reporting, trend analysis, and risk evaluation. Demonstrated ability to analyze claims utilization, identify cost drivers, and support actuarial and business decisions through clear data-driven insights. I graduated in Actuarial Science from the University of Lagos with a CGPA 4.38/5.00, where I applied predictive models like the Lee-Carter mortality model to quantify longevity risk in the Nigerian pension system. As an actuarial intern at Octamile, I worked on claims analytics, gaining practical exposure to real-world insurance data and as part of my National youth service, I mentor students in statistics and data literacy, combining technical expertise with clear communication. I am eager to contribute to data-driven decision-making in actuarial and risk analytics roles, and to grow as a professional in the field.
